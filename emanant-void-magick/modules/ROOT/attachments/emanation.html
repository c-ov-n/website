Path Numbers:

0 - Nothing

The fundamental question of metaphysics: "Why is there anything at all rather than nothing?"

Where can we start except with nothing?

Any ontology which starts with a sort of being is begging the question. We must start by interrogating the nature or lack of nature of non-being.

So let us start by diving into this void of non-being and ask what it is like.

Simply put, it is not like anything. Use the word "nothing" to points away from all things, all attributes.

Anything that comes to mind when you think of nothing, not that.

Nothing is not empty space. It has no dimensions.

Nothing is not dark. It isn't even transparent. It has no relation to light or vision.

Nothing is neither quiet nor any sound.

... but is it fair to say that nothing is not any of these? Is not the negation itself applying a sort of attribute to nothing?

00 - Unlimited

The next step in our exploration of the void before being is to realize that it is not only not anything we can think of but it is also not-not anything.

Things that have being are defined not just as they are but what they are not. Not just what they can do but also what they cannot do.

To enter into being is to become in some way limited. The nothing before being is unlimited. Anything may follow from it...

000 - All Possibility

Nothing is itself the generative force from which all being issues.

Nothing by simply being no-thing is not limited to remain nothing.

Void, by being unlimited, permits all being, all possibility to occur.

All-possibility is still a sort of non-being, still nothing. Various possibilities are in conflict and contradiction.

To continue we must move into the seed of being...

0⋮1 - Chaos

Chaos, the principle of being without reason.

Things come into being simply because nothing prevents this. Coming into being is a constant force arising from the instability of non-being. Chaos inherits this instability and only by chance evolves beyond it.

The physics of quantum mechanics elucidate this principle. For example, the double-slit experiment shows the principle of superposition, that physical systems can be in an indeterminate state that spontaneously resolves to a determinate state when measured. In as much as the system is non-determined is the principle of all-possibility in non-being. Assuming a specific unpredictable state upon measurement is this most fundamental principle of Chaos.

0⋮1 - Chaos : Primal Fire

Elementally this is the principle of fire in its highest state.

Non-being may be imagined as an infinite ocean. It stretches out in all directions in darkness but is stirred from its depth by a mysterious force. Waves ripple out and interact throwing up a fine spray that phosphoresce in the darkness. These tiny sparks depart from the void for a moment, but within each spark may be a moment, a life, a civilization, or an entire universe. Even the vastest span of being is nothing next to the void which birthed it and awaits its return.

1⋮2 - Process

Process is the principle that being which emanates from Chaos remains incomplete, containing and in communication with the void which birthed it. Within being, non-being becomes a thing in relation: a space, a possibility.

This connection to void allows emanation to continue by holding space for possibility. Everything has being by its ability to be in relation to other things. That one thing may be above another or below, within or without. Every property is standing in relation which is preceded by the possibility held.

The continuing process of emanation is like the flow in a cosmic game of improvisation. Each being in each moment has space in which something by follow and from that something else in and endless chain or web.

1⋮2 - Process : Path Numbering

Each path is numbered twice. The first number is the emanation number and is given by the order in which the paths appear in the process of emanation. The second is the Fool's Journey number and is assigned by a sequential walk of the paths of the tree. Both numberings start at zero so in this simplest case both numbers are zero.

1⋮2 - Process : Primal Waters

Elementally Process is the principle of water in its highest state. The flow of Process is the flow of water conforming to its vessel and reforming it through its constant action.

As the spark of being departs the Void its reflection appears within the surface of the waters. The waters fade and Chaos and its reflection, Process remain. A radiant light and a radiant darkness. This dance between being an non-being carries forth every stage of emanation.

3⋮5 - Form

Within pattern there emerge nodes within it, emergent phenomena.

The numbering of the spheres is first by sequence starting with zero and second by prime number. Five is the third prime number, hence the numbering associated with this sphere. That five is the third prime is simply a fact in number. Likewise twin primes are prime numbers two apart such as five and seven. Every twin prime pair greater than these are divisible by twelve. These are just a couple of the infinite forms that occur just within the counting numbers.

A key aspect of these forms is that they occur within relation. Even a simple form such as the number five is only itself because of how it stands in relation to other numbers within the pattern of number generally.

Elementally this is the principle of earth in its highest state.

4⋮7 - Change

The flow of emanation may stop to create a static world such as the world of number, or it may create unstable dynamic worlds which constantly collapse upon themselves and regrow into the voids opened. This is the fundamental nature of change, that existence is fleeting of coming into being and falling back out of being.

Note that no longer is all connected to all. A constraint on path formation is that the emanation number of the path may not exceed the prime number of the sphere.

Might this have gone the other way? In short, yes, but it is a less stable configuration as Process and Change together are too volatile and Pattern and Form too static. 5⋮11 - Agent

Within the flow of change there may emerge self-sustaining agents, dynamic self-reinforcing systems that create a sort of homeostasis.

In the primordial soup of self-catalyzing reactions molecules that have this property multiply. In the right conditions and given enough time more complex agents emerge in the process of natural selection.

Conway's Game of Life provides a perfect example of a simple agent in the "glider". In the simple world of this game the glider is a self-replicating pattern that moves itself across the landscape. It is a self-sustaining and replicating form sustaining itself through change.

10⋮29 - Soul

Up until now all of the steps of emanation can be understood as solipsistic. Manifestation itself is inherently subjective, but it isn't experienced as subjective until the agent is subsumed into the objective. The encoding of the agent itself into objective manifestation is the Soul. Soul is created by interaction of the process of void emanation with matter.

Attaining a soul makes the agent a thing in the world. It makes it a self able to be itself in communication with other selves. Becoming matter ensouled, spirit embodied, the agent transcends itself to become one among others.
