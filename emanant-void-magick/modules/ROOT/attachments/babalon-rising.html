What is Emanant Void Magick?

  • A narrative ontology which posits a theory for the nature of reality.
  • An open framework of associations for building magical systems.
  • Postmodern, neo-pythagorean, quasi-platonic, arithmological numerical philosophy.
  • Not syncretic, but adaptable.
  • Not Qabalah!

Why Emanant Void Magick?

  • Why not just use Qabalah?
    • Syncretic systems are a mess (see Joseph Campbell etc).
    • Can't express everything we want to express.
    • So many variants already (see Achad, Grant, etc)
    • Hebrew associations make it difficult to freely remix
  • EVM is by design open with only associations you want to use.
