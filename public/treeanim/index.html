0 - Nothing

The fundamental question of metaphysics: "Why is there anything at all rather than nothing?"

In order to answer this question we only need to understand the nature of nothingness. Nothing is the idea that cannot be conceived because any conception of it makes it something rather than nothing.

00 - Without Limit

A pure conception of nothingness also excludes the posibility of any limit. Nothing is not constrained from becoming not-nothing.

000 - All Possibility

This pure conception of nothingness is indistinguishable from the concept of all possibilities at once. Nothing is constantly becoming. Non-being is perfectly unstable.

These three concepts all elaborations of the same. All possibility is not manifest because it contains contradictions. We move into a possibility for it to manifest.

1 - Chaos

Existence manifests from non-being for no reason and for no purpose. This is the divine light or divine fire of manifestation, pure chaos. This level of being is pure, unreflective, uncomplicated. The manifestation of chaos is simply pure being itself, the smallest. This sort of being is also unstable. In the void there is manifestation and anihilation constantly churning unseen. For manifestation to take hold and remain it must do more.

2 - Process

Manifestation of chaos is essentially incomplete. Anything that manifests in this way leaves open the possibility of something else to follow. Manifestation following manifestation creates a flow of process. This is the fundamental principle of water.

3 - Pattern

A flow of process can be united by a pattern that describes the flow and summarizes its activity and anticipates where it will go. This creates a unity of disparate elements. The many become one while remaining their own identities.

4 - Being

The next step in manifestation is the appearance of things. Nodes in the pattern. The four elements.

5 - Time

The flow of time. The descent of fire. The emergence of destruction.

5 - The Pentagram

The five elements.

6 - Agent

Things which organize manifestation. Time becomes Will.
